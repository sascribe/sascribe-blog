## Step-by-Step: Brand Setup to First Ad Download

Here's the fastest path through the platform. Most users report completing this workflow in under 15 minutes.

### 1. Create Your Brand Profile

The AI can automatically extract your brand's name, logo, colors, and fonts from a scanned website URL. The AI suggests up to three brand colors automatically from your uploaded logo. You don't need a brand guideline PDF — just your URL and a logo file. This brand profile ensures every creative you generate stays visually consistent.

### 2. Connect Your Ad Accounts (Optional but Recommended)

Integrations allow you to connect your ad accounts to your brands on AdCreative AI. Linking platforms like Meta, Google Ads, or LinkedIn allows the AI to analyze past campaign data for better recommendations. This step feeds the algorithm your historical winners and losers, making its predictions more accurate for *your* audience — not just generic benchmarks.

### 3. Generate Your Creatives

Click "Ad Creatives" from your dashboard. Choose your ad format — social creatives, display banners, or video ads. AdCreative AI supports various ad types including social media posts, display banners, and video ads. Select your target platform sizes — Facebook, Instagram, Google Display, LinkedIn, and more. Upload your product image or choose from over 100 million free stock images. Add your headline and product description, then hit Generate.

### 4. Sort by Conversion Score and Download Winners

This is arguably the most valuable feature. Every image generated comes with a "Confidence Score" (from 1 to 100). The AI predicts how well that specific design will perform before you even spend a dollar on ads. Prioritizing creatives with a score of 90+ actually lowered Cost Per Click (CPC) in testing. Download your top-scoring variants and push them directly to your connected ad accounts.

By analyzing this data, you can identify strategies that work and stay one step ahead. This is genuinely useful for creative inspiration before you run your own generation batch.

**AI Text Generator:** One of the pleasant surprises is the built-in text generator. You don't need to be a professional copywriter. **Compliance Checker AI:** Available on Professional plans and above, Compliance Checker AI flags regulatory issues before you hit publish. If you're in finance, health, or alcohol advertising, this alone can prevent costly policy violations.

You can generate unlimited creatives, but credits are only consumed when you download them. Generate as many as you want — you only pay when you download.

## Honest Concerns You Should Know About

Transparency matters. Reviewers largely say AdCreative AI is fast, easy to use, and useful for producing many ad variations quickly. People often praise its brand setup and editing workflow, though several note outputs can feel generic or repetitive. The biggest criticism is not the creative tool itself but billing and support.

**Trial-to-paid transition:** AdCreative AI offers a 7-day free trial with 10 credits. You get full access to all platform features during the trial period. A credit card is required to activate it, but you won't be charged until the trial ends. Set a calendar reminder for day 6. If you decide it's not for you, cancel before the trial expires — the platform says you can do it in two clicks.

**Customization limits:** Some users note a common limitation in customization options, wishing for more control over design elements. If you need pixel-level control over typography and layout, treat the outputs as strong starting points and refine in Figma or Photoshop.

**Video features require Professional plan:** Real AI UGC Videos are locked behind extremely expensive plans starting from $249. If video is your primary need and budget is tight, evaluate whether a dedicated video tool is a better fit for now.
