## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can AdCreative AI replace a graphic designer entirely?
For routine ad creatives—display ads, social media ads, basic variations—it can handle the bulk of the work. For brand-level design, landing pages, or complex creative projects, you'll still want a human designer. Think of it as replacing repetitive creative tasks, not your entire design function.

### What ad platforms does AdCreative AI support?
It generates creatives optimized for Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google Display Network, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Twitter/X. Creatives are sized and formatted for each platform automatically.

### Is AdCreative AI worth it for small budgets?
Yes, arguably more so. When your ad budget is limited, every dollar of creative spend matters more. Getting higher-performing creatives without paying for freelance design work means more of your budget goes toward actual ad spend.

### How does the conversion scoring actually work?
The platform's AI is trained on a massive dataset of ad creatives and their performance metrics. It scores each generated creative based on predicted click-through and conversion rates, so you can prioritize the top-scoring options before spending anything on distribution.

### Does it work for video ads?
AdCreative AI is primarily focused on static and animated display creatives. If video is your primary ad format, you'll need a complementary tool for that. However, for the majority of display and social ad placements, static creatives remain a core format in 2026.
