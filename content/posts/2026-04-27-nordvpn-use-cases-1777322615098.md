## Torrenting and P2P File Sharing With Dedicated Servers

Torrenting remains a legitimate use-case for Linux ISOs, open-source software, creative commons content, and large academic datasets. The issue most users face is ISP throttling and IP exposure.

Torrenting is permitted on all P2P-optimized servers (clearly marked in-app), with no bandwidth throttling detected in sustained 8-hour BitTorrent tests. In torrenting-specific benchmarks, NordVPN achieved 538 Mbps download speeds while connected to a 1 GB line within the UK.

Reddit users have raised a valid concern: NordVPN does not offer port forwarding — a hard requirement for self-hosted services like game servers, torrent seedboxes, or home automation bridges. This omission is deliberate and consistent across all plans. If port forwarding is essential to your workflow, this is a genuine limitation to consider.

### Best Configuration for Torrenting

1. Open NordVPN and navigate to specialty servers
2. Select **P2P** servers — these are optimized for peer-to-peer traffic
3. Enable the **Kill Switch** to prevent IP leaks if the VPN connection drops
4. Use the **NordLynx** protocol for the best balance of speed and security

The Kill Switch feature automatically cuts off your internet if the VPN drops, preventing accidental data leaks — especially useful when switching Wi-Fi networks or dealing with unstable connections.

## Travel Security: Public Wi-Fi, Censorship, and Banking Access

One of the most underrated nordvpn use-cases 2026 is travel security. Airport lounges, hotel Wi-Fi, café networks — these are all hunting grounds for man-in-the-middle attacks.

NordVPN's headquarters are in Panama, a country that isn't part of data-sharing alliances, and Panama is particularly robust when it comes to data privacy laws. This matters when you're connecting from countries with aggressive surveillance.

### Bypassing Censorship in Restrictive Countries

NordVPN provides obfuscation via obfuscated servers in 16 countries (the U.S., the U.K., Canada, Japan, Poland, Spain, etc.). You need to switch to the OpenVPN protocol to access the obfuscated server list. This will slow down your connection more than other protocols.

For even more robust censorship circumvention, NordVPN introduced its **NordWhisper** protocol. Introduced in January 2025, the protocol is based on the Tor Project's WebTunnel technology. It wraps your encrypted VPN traffic in a standard HTTPS connection to hide it from detection.

### Protecting Banking Access Abroad

When you travel, logging into your bank from an unfamiliar IP can trigger fraud alerts and account lockouts. You can add your dedicated IP to banking, email, or work VPN whitelists, which avoids CAPTCHAs and suspicious login alerts. The Dedicated IP add-on gives you the same IP every time you connect, preventing the constant churn of shared VPN addresses from triggering security systems.
