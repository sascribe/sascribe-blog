## The Appier Acquisition: What It Means for Users


Appier acquired France-based AdCreative.ai for $38.7 million (including a $27.3 million base) in early 2025, accelerating its Generative AI-driven advertising and marketing innovation.
 
Founded in 2021 and trusted by global brands like Snapchat, Pernod Ricard, Reckitt, and BNP Paribas, the platform merges AI technology with advertising expertise.


So what does this mean day-to-day? 
The vision is a seamless pipeline where businesses can use Appier's AIRIS platform to identify high-potential customer segments and instantly generate tailored ad creatives through AdCreative.ai's tools.
 For now, the product remains standalone. But Appier's enterprise data infrastructure suggests deeper audience intelligence integrations are coming — a meaningful upgrade for performance marketers.

## Pricing in 2026: What You'll Actually Pay


The Starter plan costs $29 a month for Startups, providing 10 credits a month for one user and unlocking all key features of the platform.
 
The Premium plan costs $59 per month for startups with 25 credits for up to two brands, or $249 a month for Professionals with 200 credits for up to 10 users.
 
The Ultimate plan costs $99 a month for Startups with 50 credits and two users, or $399 a month for Professionals.


Here's the critical detail most users miss: 
AdCreative.ai's credit system isn't one-credit-per-creative. A basic generation with minimal AI enhancement consumes 1 credit, but request premium AI copywriting, and that same creative now costs 2–3 credits.
 
Credits don't roll over, and the best pricing requires annual commitment.


If you're running five or more campaigns monthly, [start with the 7-day free trial](https://sascribe.com/go/adcreative) and track your actual credit consumption before committing to an annual plan.

## The Real User Complaints: What To Watch Out For

No AdCreative AI coverage in 2026 is complete without addressing the billing concerns that dominate review platforms. 
The biggest criticism is not the creative tool itself but billing and support: multiple users report unwanted charges, poor trial communication, refund delays, and trust issues.



Across review platforms like G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit, marketers have voiced growing concerns over delayed support responses, unresolved tickets, and even billing disputes during the trial period.
 
Many reviewers feel the pricing is too high, especially for small businesses and freelancers, and report issues with the free trial, unexpected charges, and difficulties obtaining refunds. Some users indicate that the number of credits in each package is insufficient.


**Our advice:** Use a virtual card or pre-paid method for the free trial. Set a calendar reminder for day 6. And document everything if you need to request a cancellation.

On the positive side, 
on G2, AdCreative AI holds a solid 4.2 out of 5 rating, with users praising its ability to quickly generate ad variants and its predictive scoring feature, the Creative Performance Score.
 
Multiple Trustpilot reviewers report that AdCreative.ai has genuinely transformed how they approach A/B testing and creative variation, with the platform's ability to understand what works across different niches leading to significant improvements in campaign performance.


## Does the ROI Still Hold Up?

For the right user — an e-commerce brand or agency running high-volume Meta and Google campaigns — AdCreative AI can meaningfully reduce creative production time. 
For performance marketers who prioritize conversion optimization over creative flexibility, AdCreative.ai represents excellent value in 2026.


But if you need granular design control, video-first workflows, or you're running a budget under $50/month on ads,

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is AdCreative AI legit or a scam?

AdCreative AI is a legitimate platform — 
businesses in more than 194 countries rely on AdCreative.ai
, and it's now owned by publicly traded Appier (TSE: 4180). However, billing complaints around auto-renewals and refund delays are real and well-documented across G2, Trustpilot, and Reddit. Use a virtual card for the trial and cancel early if it's not for you.

### How many ad creatives can I make on the $29/month plan?


The Starter plan gives you 10 monthly credits, 1 brand, and 1 user seat. Every AI asset type is unlocked, including Unlimited Generations, Text Generator AI, and Ad Platform Integrations.
 You can generate unlimited *previews* — credits are only consumed when you download or export the final creative.

### Is AdCreative AI better than Canva for ads?

They serve different purposes. 
AdCreative AI is not a drag-and-drop editor like Canva — it's a data processing engine
 focused on conversion-optimized output. Canva gives you more design freedom, while AdCreative AI prioritises speed and predictive performance scoring. For paid ad campaigns at scale, AdCreative AI has the edge; for general design work, Canva is more versatile.
