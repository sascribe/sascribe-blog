## What is Sascribe?

Sascribe is an independent software review publication. We test, compare, and analyze AI tools, SaaS platforms, and marketing software so you can make faster, smarter purchasing decisions.

We have no preferred vendors. We earn a commission if you purchase through our links — but that never influences our recommendations. If a tool isn't worth your money, we say so.

## What We Cover

- **AI Marketing Tools** — ad creative generation, copywriting, design automation
- **AI Voice and Video** — text-to-speech, video generation, avatar tools
- **Newsletter Platforms** — email marketing, audience growth, monetization
- **Privacy and Security** — VPNs, password managers, security software

## Our Review Process

Every tool we review is evaluated against the same criteria: actual use case fit, pricing transparency, competitor comparison, and support reliability. We update reviews when pricing changes or our assessment changes.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Sascribe participates in affiliate programs. When you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Our affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial assessments.
