## Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sascribe.com collects, uses and protects information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

**Email Address**
If you subscribe to our newsletter we collect your email address. This is used solely to send you our newsletter and related communications. We use Beehiiv to manage our email list.

**Usage Data**
We may collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent on site through Cloudflare analytics. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

## How We Use Your Information

- To send our newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
- To improve our website and content
- To analyze traffic patterns and popular content

## Cookies

Sascribe.com uses minimal cookies necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

## Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

- **Beehiiv** — Email newsletter management. View their privacy policy at beehiiv.com
- **Cloudflare** — DNS, CDN and security. View their privacy policy at cloudflare.com
- **Netlify** — Website hosting. View their privacy policy at netlify.com

## Affiliate Links

Some links on our site are affiliate links. Clicking these links does not result in any additional data collection. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more details.

## Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email. To request deletion of your data contact us at hello@sascribe.com.
