## Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sascribe.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

**Email Address**
If you subscribe to our newsletter we collect your email address. This is used to send you our newsletter and related communications. We use Beehiiv to manage our email list.

**Usage Data**
We collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent on site through Cloudflare Analytics and Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager). This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

## How We Use Your Information

- To send our newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
- To improve our website and content
- To analyze traffic patterns and popular content
- To attribute conversions when visitors arrive via paid advertising

## Cookies and Tracking

Sascribe.com uses cookies and tracking technologies for the following purposes:

- **Essential cookies** — required for basic site functionality
- **Analytics cookies** — Google Tag Manager and Cloudflare Analytics measure traffic and engagement (anonymized, aggregated)
- **Advertising and conversion tracking** — when you arrive from a paid traffic source (such as PropellerAds), our tracking platform (BeMob) sets a session identifier to attribute conversions to the originating ad. These identifiers are not used to build user profiles or for cross-site behavioral targeting.
- **Email marketing** — when you subscribe to our newsletter (via Beehiiv) we record your email and basic engagement metrics (opens, clicks)

You can opt out of analytics tracking by enabling Do Not Track in your browser. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email.

## Third Party Services

We use the following third party services. Each handles your data under their own privacy policy:

- **Cloudflare** — DNS, CDN, hosting (Cloudflare Pages), and analytics. https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
- **Google Tag Manager** — analytics tag management. https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Beehiiv** — email newsletter management. https://www.beehiiv.com/privacy
- **BeMob** — affiliate conversion tracking for paid traffic campaigns. https://bemob.com/privacy-policy
- **PropellerAds** — paid traffic source for some visitors arriving at our landing pages. https://propellerads.com/privacy/
- **Amazon Associates** — affiliate program for product recommendations linking to Amazon. https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GX7NJQ4ZB8MHFRNJ

## Affiliate Links

Some links on our site are affiliate links. Clicking these links does not result in any additional personal data collection beyond what is described in this policy. See our [Affiliate Disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/) for more details.

## Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

You have the right to:
- Know what personal data we collect about you
- Request a copy of your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of email communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@sascribe.com.

## Data Retention

We retain newsletter subscriber data until you unsubscribe. Anonymous analytics data is retained per the policies of the underlying providers (Cloudflare, Google).
